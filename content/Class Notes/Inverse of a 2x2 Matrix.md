> [!example] Inverse of a 2x2 Matrix  
> The inverse of  
> $$A=\begin{bmatrix}a&b\\c&d\end{bmatrix}\text{ is }A^{-1}=\frac{1}{ad-bc}\begin{bmatrix}d&-b\\-c&a\end{bmatrix}$$  

**Remark:** A $2 \times 2$ matrix is invertible iff $ad-bc\neq 0$.

> [!definition] Subset of R^n  
> A subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$ is a collection of elements of $\mathbb{R}^n$. The symbol $\subset$ denotes this relationship: $X\subset \mathbb{R}^n$ means that $X$ is a subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$.

> [!definition] Codomain  
> The **codomain** of a [[Function as Rule|function]] is its target space.  
  
*Example:* In the equation $f:X\to Y$, the codomain is $Y$, which could be $\mathbb{R}$, $\mathbb{C}$, or any other set.

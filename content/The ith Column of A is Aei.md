> [!proposition] The $i$th Column of $A$ is $A\vec{e}_{i}$  
  
*Example:*  
$$\begin{bmatrix}3 & -2 & 0 \\2 & 1 & 2 \\0 & 4 & 3 \\1 & 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}0 \\1 \\0\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}-2 \\1 \\4 \\0\end{bmatrix}$$

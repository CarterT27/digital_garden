> [!definition] Outcome  
> The result of an [[Experiment|experiment]].  
  
The possible outcomes of rolling a die are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6.  
  
The possible outcomes of flipping a coin twice are HH, HT, TH,  
and TT.

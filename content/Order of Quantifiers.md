> [!example] Order of Quantifiers
> The statement  
> $(\forall n\text{ integer },n\ge2)(\exists p\text{ prime })\text{ }n/p\text{ is an integer}$ is true,  
> $(\exists p\text{ prime})(\forall n\text{ integer},n\geq 2)\text{ }n/p\text{ is an integer}$ is false.

> [!definition] Arithmetic Operations  
>  
> |Operation|Operator|Example|Value|  
> |---|---|---|---|
> |Addition|`+`|`2 + 3`|`5`|  
> |Subtraction|`-`|`2 - 3`|`-1`|  
> |Multiplication|`*`|`2 * 3`|`6`|  
> |Division|`/`|`7 / 3`|`2.66667`|  
> |Remainder|`%`|`7 % 3`|`1`|  
> |Exponentiation|`**`|`2 ** 0.5`|`1.41421`|  
  
Python uses the order of operations - **PEMDAS**  
  
```py  
5 * 2 ** 3 # 40  
(5 * 2) ** 3 # 1000  
```
